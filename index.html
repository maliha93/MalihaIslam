Maliha T Islam





Hi! I'm currently a second year MS/PhD student at University of Massachusetts Amherst. I work with Professor Alexandra Meliou in the Database and Information Management Lab. I received my bachelors in Computer Science & Engineering from University of Dhaka in Bangladesh.
My research interests broadly include Database Management, Data Analytics, Algorithmic Fairness and Machine Learning. My resume can be found here.
